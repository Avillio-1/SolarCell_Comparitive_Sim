# Evidence sources

The parameter-level source, applicability, confidence, and limitation remain authoritative in
`data/calibration/parameter_registry.yaml`. This page provides the short source map.

## Field data and weather

- NREL PVDAQ, Open Energy Data Initiative, DOI
  [10.25984/1846021](https://doi.org/10.25984/1846021).
- [NASA POWER documentation](https://power.larc.nasa.gov/docs/) and
  [referencing guide](https://power.larc.nasa.gov/docs/referencing/).

## Soiling and rainfall

- Jones et al., central Saudi Arabia cleaning study,
  [10.1109/JPHOTOV.2016.2535308](https://doi.org/10.1109/JPHOTOV.2016.2535308).
- “The Impact of Soiling on PV Module Performance in Saudi Arabia,”
  [10.3390/en15218033](https://doi.org/10.3390/en15218033).
- Adinoyi and Said, long-exposure Dhahran study,
  [Renewable Energy 60:633–636](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0960148113003078).
- Bessa et al., monitoring and rain-threshold review,
  [10.1016/j.isci.2021.102165](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.isci.2021.102165).
- Kimber et al., “The effect of soiling on large grid-connected photovoltaic systems,” IEEE
  WCPEC, 2006.
- Ilse et al., global soiling economics,
  [10.1016/j.joule.2019.08.019](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.joule.2019.08.019).

## Saudi dust climate

- “Spatial and Temporal Variations in the Incidence of Dust Storms in Saudi Arabia,”
  [10.3390/geosciences9040162](https://doi.org/10.3390/geosciences9040162).
- “Synoptic characteristics of the spatial variability of spring dust storms over Saudi Arabia,”
  [Atmósfera 36(3)](https://www.scielo.org.mx/scielo.php?script=sci_arttext&pid=S0187-62362023000300124).

## Reactive inspection and cleaning

- “SolPowNet: Dust Detection on Photovoltaic Panels,”
  [10.3390/electronics14214230](https://doi.org/10.3390/electronics14214230).
- “Deep Learning-Based Dust Detection on Solar Panels,”
  [10.3390/su16198664](https://doi.org/10.3390/su16198664).
- [DJI Matrice 350 RTK specifications](https://enterprise.dji.com/matrice-350-rtk/specs).

Curated image benchmarks provide a performance ceiling, not a field confusion matrix. Cleaning
throughput and water assumptions remain provisional.

## Coating, cooling, and water

- Anti-soiling coating review,
  [10.3390/ma15207139](https://doi.org/10.3390/ma15207139).
- KAUST-inspired multifunctional coating study,
  [10.1002/eem2.70350](https://doi.org/10.1002/eem2.70350).
- Electrostatic dust-removal context,
  [10.1126/sciadv.abm0078](https://doi.org/10.1126/sciadv.abm0078).

Reported favorable-condition endpoints are used only in labeled research calibrations. They do not
establish Riyadh field lifetime, installed cost, or annual water yield.

## Economics

- [Saudi Electricity Company tariffs](https://www.se.com.sa/en-us/Customers/Pages/TariffRates.aspx).
- [Sakaka solar project](https://www.vision2030.gov.sa/en/explore/projects/sakaka-solar-power-plant)
  for utility PPA context.

- IRENA, “The cost of financing for renewable power,”
  [2023 report](https://www.irena.org/-/media/Files/IRENA/Agency/Publication/2023/May/IRENA_The_cost_of_financing_renewable_power_2023.pdf).

The registry's literature-v3 source review was recorded on 2026-07-18. Target-project quotes and
contracts should supersede generic ranges when available.
