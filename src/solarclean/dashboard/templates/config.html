What the location changes
A saved map position sets site.latitude, site.longitude, both timezone fields, and DST-correct whole-day period offsets (00:00–23:00); the location's IANA timezone is detected automatically from the coordinates. With nasa_power, it also changes the fetched and cached hourly GHI/DNI/DHI, air temperature, wind, humidity, and precipitation. Rainfall, seasonal soiling, event dates, and cleaning decisions then follow that weather and local-time grouping; humidity-coupled soiling (dew cementation) and coating condensation follow the fetched weather when enabled.
Calibration scope
Changing the map location updates site, timezone, and weather inputs; it does not automatically create a site-specific dust or cost calibration. This configuration keeps its named calibration.assumption_set until those assumptions are deliberately edited and supported with local evidence.
