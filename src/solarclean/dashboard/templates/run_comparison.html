What is this?

Reconciliation is the run's self-audit. Before any ranking is accepted, the backend checks that all three scenarios used the same weather and event data, that annual totals equal the sum of the daily results, and that costs match the operational quantities the simulation actually recorded — for example, the crew labour cost must equal crew hours × the labour rate, and the water cost must equal water used × the water price. If any check fails, the run is not trusted and no winner is declared.
